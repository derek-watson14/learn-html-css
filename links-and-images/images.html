Images

This page covers common image formats, but you may also be looking for links and useful extras

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos with large color spectrum, but they disallow transparent pixels

GIFs

GIFs are good for animations. More limited color pallete than JPG, and limited in transparency (unlike PNGS)

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons. They require more memory to contain detail equivilent to an identical JPG. But they handle opacity well and have no color limitations.

SVGs

SVGs are amazing. Use them wherever you can. They scale to any dimension with no loss of quality. Good for everything PNGs are good for.
